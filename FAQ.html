FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


November 21th & 22th      10 Events      Gateways-2020

Register Now

Do I need to register to be able to take part in events?

Yes, you must register at TechMerise to be able to take part in events.

Do I need to be a College Student to register?

Yes, you must be a College student in order to be able to register.

Students from what courses are allowed to register?

Students from only Computer Background are allowed to participate. Courses like BCA, BSc. CS, BSc. IT, BTech CS, MCA, MSc. CS, MSc. IT, MTech CS, etc.

Do I have to pay to Register?

You don't have to pay to register, however you must pay to be able to register for events in the User Panel.

Where can I register?

You can click on the Register button on the top right corner, or you can click here to register.

What is the registration process?

  1. First, you must register by entering the details given in the registration form.
  2. Once you have registered, you can go to the login page and enter the email and password that you registered with.
  3. After logging in, you will be prompted to confirm your details. Remember that once you confirm your details you cannot change them later. You must make sure the details you entered match with your college credentials.
  4. Once you have confirmed your details you will have to upload an Identification document. Accepted documents: College ID Card, Any official college document, receipts, report-cards, screenshot of college portal with your information visible, etc. It is necessary for us to verify your information so that there is a fair competition and regulation in unfair registrations.
  5. Once you have completed the identification process, you can wait while our Student Team will verify your account. You will be notified through an email when your account is verified.

My account verification got accepted, what do I do?

Congratulations, you are now able to register for events. To be able to register for events, you must first load balance into your TechMerise account. Browse to the Payments section of FAQ to get more details.

My account verification got rejected, what do I do?

Your account might have gotten rejected because the details could not be matched or because you are not eligible to participate in the fest. If you think this is an error, then please contact us. You can find contact details on the Contact Us Page.

I forgot my Password

Write us a mail at [email protected] with the E-mail that you registered with and we will help you out!

How much do I have to pay to participant in an event?

You must pay Rs. 100 for each event that you wish to participate in. If you wish to participate in all the 6 events you must pay Rs. 600 accordingly.

Do I need to pay for every event individually?

You don't have to pay for each event individually. You can load balance into your TechMerise account at once and then use it to register in any event that you wish.

How will amount be credited to my TechMerise account?

  1. First you must pay through either of the payment methods available on the Balance page manually.
  2. Once you have made the payment, you have to fill the form on the Balance page with details like Transaction ID (or UTR Code), Payment Method, etc.
  3. Once you have filled the form, our accounting team will verify the payment with the Transaction ID that you have inserted and you will be credited the money into your TechMerise Account.
  4. You can review the Payment Status after submitting the form.

What are the different Payment Methods?

The different payment methods are Google Pay, PayTM, Bank Transfer.

My Payment Form got Verified, what now?

Congratulations, the amount would have been credited to your TechMerise account. You can now use that to register for events.

My Payment Form got Rejected, why?

Your payment form might have gotten rejected because the payment could not have been matched with the details you entered. Do not worry, if you think this is an error please create a Support Ticket under Accounting Queries after Logging in.

My Payment Form got partially Remunerated, why?

Your payment form might have gotten partially remunerated because the amount received at TechMerise would have been lower than the amount you submitted in the form. If you think this is an error then please create a Support Ticker under Accounting Queries after logging in.

Can I get a refund?

No, refunds will not be processed.

I need additional assistance, who do I contact?

You can create a support ticket under Accounting Queries after logging in or you can also call any member of the core team for additional assistance.

How many events are there?

There are a total of 6 events. They are Coding and Debugging, IT Quiz, Start Up Pitch, Graphic Designing, Fast Typing, Short Film Making. Go over to the Events page to get more information.

What are the prizes for each event?

Prizes are as follows:
1st Place: ₹2500
2nd Place: ₹1500
3rd Place: ₹1000
Winners also have a chance to win exciting goodies.

When will the winners be announced?

The winners will be announced in the Valedictory Ceremony (1:30 PM - 2:15 PM Day 2). It will be live streamed in the Home Page of the website. The winners will also be shared in the Winners page of the Website.

Where will the events take place?

All the Events will take place under the domain "www.techmerise.com" itself, through the User Portal.

How do I register for events?

First, make sure you have the necessary amount loaded into your TechMerise account to be able to register for the event. Browse over to the Payments section of FAQ to get more clarity into this.

If you have the necessary amount, go over to the Events page and click on "Register". You will be registered in the event and will be contacted by our team soon.

Where will the Events take place?

The schedule can be found at the home page of the website.

How will I receive the prize if I win?

If you are a winner, we will reward you the prize via Bank Transfer and send you the E-certificate after contacting you.

Do participants get certificates?

Yes, the participants who have registered in one or more events will be able to download E-Certificates through the User Portal.

How will the event co-ordinators communicate with me?

Once you have registered in the events, our Event Co-ordinators will personally call you and provide you the link to join our Telegram group. All official information will be shared in the group. Make sure you have enabled notifications for it.

I was not able to attend the event on time, can I participate again?

No, you will not be allowed to participate again.

No, you will not be allowed to participate again.
